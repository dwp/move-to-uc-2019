Your current benefits are ending soon

Dear Joe

Your current benefits are ending soon, so in 1 month we’ll ask you to move to Universal Credit. You must not move yet as it could affect your payments, but there are some things you need to do now to get ready to move.

What you need to do now

Make sure you’ve got:

  • a current telephone number - if it’s changed recently, please tell your work coach or call 01642 417 516
  • your own personal email address
  • your own bank account

Get together evidence of your:

  • housing costs - like rent, mortgage payments or service charges
  • childcare costs
  • earnings if you’re in work
  • savings or other income

Find out more about Universal Credit

You can find out more about Universal Credit at www.gov.uk/universalcredit or search for ‘Universal Credit’.

Get help with the internet

To get Universal Credit you’ll need an online account, so if you need help getting online or setting up an email address, your jobcentre can give you access to a computer and help you.

Contact us on 01642 417 516 to ask for help.

What happens next

We’ll contact you again in 1 month and ask you to move to Universal Credit. Until then, please continue to:

  • tell us about any changes to your circumstances
  • attend any appointments you have booked

If you do not, you may be paid more or less Universal Credit than you should.
