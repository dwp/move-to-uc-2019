Get help with the internet

If you need help getting online or using the internet, your jobcentre can give you access to a computer and help you make a claim.

Contact us on 01442 306 799 to ask for help.

What you’ll need

You’ll need:

  • a current telephone number
  • your own personal email address
  • your own bank account

You’ll also need evidence of:

  • housing costs - like rent, mortgage payments or service charges
  • childcare costs
  • earnings if you’re in work
  • savings or other income
  • any medical conditions or fit notes

Until you’ve moved

Until you’ve completed your Universal Credit claim, please continue to:

  • tell us about any changes to your circumstances
  • attend any appointments you have booked

If you do not, you may be paid more or less Universal Credit than you should.
